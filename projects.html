FICO Loan Prediction Model

March 2024

Analyzed key trends and patterns in loan approvals using FICO dataset with 617,996 instances. Explored factors including debt-to-income ratio (DTI), loan amount, race, gender, and income. Achieved significant insights: 94.3% approval for Female pairs, 92.5% for White applicants, and optimal DTI range of 60-80%.

California Housing Affordability Trends

2017-2022

Analyzed California housing affordability trends using American Community Survey (ACS) data, comparing 2017 vs 2022. Key findings include a 3.5% increase in mortgage burden, with over 65% of households in low-income brackets. Study revealed significant affordability gaps across racial groups and proposed a $17 million Housing Support Program (HSP).
